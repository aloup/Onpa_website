Kanye’s New Opera Nebuchadnezzar
Live Stream Announced

It premieres at the Hollywood Bowl in Los Angeles on Sunday night

by by Noah Yoo,Staff Writer

Kanye West recently detailed his first opera, Nebuchadnezzar which is “based on the biblical story of the Babylonian King from the Book of Daniel.” It is set to premiere tomorrow, November 24, at the Hollywood Bowl in Los Angeles at 4 p.m. PT/7 p.m. ET. Now, it’s been announced that Tidal will be live streaming the performance tomorrow—find more details here.

Italian performance artist and longtime West collaborator Vanessa Beecroft directed Nebuchadnezzar, which is set to feature West’s Sunday Service collective, the singer Peter Collins, and the group Infinity’s Song. His latest album Jesus Is King was released in October.
